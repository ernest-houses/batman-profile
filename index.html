Bruce Wayne

Businessman · The Dark Knight

By day, I’m the CEO of Wayne Enterprises, one of the largest conglomerates in Gotham City. By night, I fight crime as Batman, using my intellect, detective skills, and advanced technology to protect the city from criminals.

I’ve spent years honing my body and mind to the peak of human potential, mastering various forms of martial arts and criminology to serve as Gotham’s protector.

On the personal side, I enjoy working on new gadgets in the Batcave, maintaining Wayne Manor, and occasionally patrolling the city with my allies Robin and Alfred.
